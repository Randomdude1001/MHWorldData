            Beotodus is a Large Monster in Monster Hunter World (MHW) ブラントドス (Burantodosu) in Japanese. It was added with the Iceborne Expansion on Sept 6th 2019. 

             

            A piscine wyvern that uses the sharp fin on its head to cut through the frozen soil, swimming freely through the snow.
            Can often be seen hurling snow at its prey using its leg fins."

            Beotodus Details & Locations

            • Found in: Hoarfrost Reach
            • Target of Quests: By Our Powers Combined
            • Is hostile when at very close range
            • Attacks other monsters: Has turf war with Banbaro 
            • Small Monsters version: n/a
            • Size: ♕ ??  ~ ♕ ??

            • HP (Tempered): ~??

             

            Beotodus Combat Info

            A snow-shark of sorts that can swim freely through the ground and emerge for agile and devastating takedowns. It inhabits areas where snow is deep, which slows hunter movement. Hunters can use Sonic Bombs to force it to surface.

            • Deals physical and Ice damage, and can inflict Iceblight
            • Weak to traps and Blast Damage.
            • Resistant to Ice and Dragon damage.
            • Kinsect Extract:
              • Red: Head
              • Orange: Back
              • White: Tail
            • Enraged mode: ??
            • You can obtain a Shiny Drops by attacking its tail.
